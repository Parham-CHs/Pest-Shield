About Us

Skedaddle Humane Wildlife Control was founded in 1989 with the mission of providing homeowners with humane and effective solutions to their wildlife conflicts. Since then we’ve helped thousands of clients across North America rid their homes of unwanted wildlife visitors using safe and humane methods.

With three decades of experience under our belt, our team of skilled technicians is equipped with the knowledge and tools necessary to safely and humanely remove wildlife from your property. Whether it’s a mischievous raccoon, a noisy squirrel, or a family of birds seeking shelter, we approach each situation with care and professionalism.

Owning a home comes with all sorts of unexpected challenges and having a family of raccoons in the attic or mice in the kitchen can be both stressful and overwhelming. That’s why we’ve made it our mission to provide prompt, reliable, and courteous service to every client, every time. Our full-service wildlife removal solutions take the stress out of the situation and give you the peace of mind of knowing your property is safe and secured for years to come. From the very beginning, our objective has been to provide client-focused solutions that keep your home and family safe without doing harm to the wild animals we share our communities with.

To deliver on our promise to clients we take a hands-on approach to wildlife removal. Our trained staff go where the animals go, climbing up ladders, crawling through attics and squeezing below decks to humanely remove wildlife, clean up the mess they’ve left behind and secure their entry points. We do all this while making sure we always answer your calls, show up on time and clean up after the job’s done.

It all starts when you connect with our friendly and knowledgeable customer service team to schedule an initial property assessment. Our experience has taught us that behind every successful wildlife removal is a thorough understanding of the problem and a well thought out game plan. From the moment you contact us, you can expect personalized attention and expert guidance tailored to your unique situation.

Once our team has had a chance to inspect, we’ll take the time to walk you through our findings and service options. Whether it’s explaining our process, providing detailed quotes, or answering your questions, we strive to exceed your expectations at every turn. We believe in transparency, honesty, and integrity in every interaction. You can count on us to be there throughout the process to ensure your home is free of wildlife and protected for the future. Join countless homeowners who have relied on us to solve their wildlife problems with efficiency and care.

Thank you for considering Skedaddle for your wildlife control needs. Let us take the stress out of wildlife management so you can get back to the things in your life you truly care about.

SKEDADDLE’S STORY

Skedaddle’s story began in 1989 when Founder and President Bill Dowd got into the wildlife control business with the goal of resolving client’s wildlife problems using humane methods. At that time trapping and relocating wild animals was the most common approach used by exterminators and trappers. Bill soon realized that wildlife that are trapped and relocated have slim chances of survival in their new environments and that most service providers were offering homeowners very little in the way of preventing future incidents. It was pretty clear that trapping wasn’t going to get rid of all the wild animals in the city and homeowners needed better solutions when they got too close to comfort.

Skedaddle has grown from humble beginnings, Bill’s first office was his childhood bedroom in Hamilton, Ontario. His mother was his first employee, serving as receptionist. Armed with a pager and a service van, Bill placed an ad in the phonebook and calls began to roll in from across Southern Ontario. Each day presented new and unique challenges to overcome, but each grateful client convinced Bill he was on to something.

“Since the day I got started, my mission has remained unchanged: to provide humane wildlife removal services that homeowners can trust. That trust, forged in the earliest days of our company, remains the cornerstone of everything we do. As we’ve grown, we’ve never lost sight of the importance of being there for our clients when they need us most.” said Bill Dowd, Skedaddle President & CEO.

Along the way, techniques, equipment and systems were developed to find humane solutions to all sorts of wildlife conflicts. A typical day might start with removing a raccoon family from an attic, then racing to capture a bat flying around a living room and capping it all off going face to face with a skunk burrowed below a front porch.

In just a few short years Skedaddle was ready to grow, launching new locations in Ottawa and later Montreal. By 2013, success in new markets led to further expansion through franchising with local owners and operators eager to join the Skedaddle team. Today Skedaddle provides service in over 40 locations across Ontario, Quebec, Nova Scotia, British Columbia as well as Wisconsin, Maryland and Minnesota in the United States. Skedaddle remains excited to continue growing and helping homeowners across North America.
